CHARACTER DETAILS

Learn more about the characters from the world of Sea of FTars!

ZALE

The Sunboy

Born on the Summer Solstice, Zale is optimistic and bold. He wields the Sunblade, through which he can absorb Eclipse Orbs whenever his attacks need an extra kick.

As a blade-dancer, he walks the path of the Guardian God Solen. The Elder Mist foretold that, in order to find his light and achieve his full potential, he will need to “stare at the night inside of him”.

VALERE

The Moongirl

Born on the Winter Solstice, Valere shows curiosity and balance. Gifted with Lunar Magic, she can absorb Eclipse Orbs in her Moonstaff to dish out massive damage.

Trained in the martial ways of the monk, she walks the path of the Guardian Goddess Luana. The Elder Mist sees great potential in her, believing she might one day be the one to “create paths on water”.

GARL

The Warrior-Cook

Garl was born a regular kid devoid of any special powers. Along with his training as a cook, his innately strong constitution makes him a capable defensive fighter, giving him the tools to support his two best friends in their Solstice Warrior duties.

The Elder Mist saw something peculiar in the stars about him, as it seemed his heart just may be warm enough to “soothe a long tormented soul”.

LICH

The Fleshmancer

An evil alchemist who used the power of alchemy that stops her aging and makes her nearly immortal. Lich has been studying and researching alchemy for her entire life until one day, her experiment was ruined by some mischievous and irresponsible people, causing her to lose everything she had. Since then, her hate for humanity kept growing and now she plans to bring great destruction to the land.

Known as the Fleshmancer, Lich also possesses the power to take the souls of the dead and bringing them back to life as her servants.

LOBELIA

The Magician of Disaster

The Fleshmancer’s right hand man. Lobelia is a talented mage capable of using destructive sound wave magic. Lobelia may seem like a kind and humble man up front, but deep inside he is a cruel mage who find joy from the sound of destruction.

After striking a deal with the Fleshmancer, he is now dedicated to helping his master achieve her goals, and he will not hesitate to destroy anything or anyone in his or his master’s way.

NIER

The Girl of Death

A young girl who often follows the Fleshmancer around. Shy and hesitant, not much is known about her past. Nier seem to have a unique power that allows her to take life force away from living creatures. However, she still could not fully control her powers that any drastic emotional change causes her to lose control of her powers and cause terrible consequences.
